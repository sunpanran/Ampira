Get news from bookmarked sites, then let AI organize and explain it.

Bookmarks are easy to save and easy to forget. The sites you follow are scattered across the web, and checking each one every day takes time.

Install from the Chrome Web Store ↗

Ampira reads the bookmarks you choose, brings saved pages and new posts from those sites into one New Tab page, and sorts them by time or importance. Optional AI creates card summaries and a daily brief. It can also read an article and answer follow-up questions.

If you track an industry or research topic over time, put the sources you follow in one bookmark folder. Ampira keeps their updates together, sorts them by importance, and uses AI to create a daily brief. Open any article to ask follow-up questions.

Ampira works in Chrome and Microsoft Edge. Data stays local by default, and no account is required. It has no ads, behavioral analytics, or developer-operated backend.
