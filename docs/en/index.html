简体中文 繁體中文 English

Distill bookmarks into daily signals.

Ampira is a local-first New Tab extension for Chrome and Microsoft Edge. It reads the bookmarks and public news sources you choose, then organizes a feed, reading queue, archive, and optional AI summaries inside your browser.

Ampira has no advertising, analytics, developer-operated backend, or account system.
